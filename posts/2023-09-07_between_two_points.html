between two points

september 7, 2023

the human psyche continues to confuse me.

by most metrics, i'm probably okay. i have plenty of friends. i'm working out now, diet is going well. i'm travelling soon. my family is supportive of me.

even emotionally, which tends to be my biggest struggle on a day-to-day, i don't even feel distressed like i have in the past few days. i am arguably feeling ok - i'm not spiralling like usual.

...

yet at the same time...

i don't want people to notice me.

i don't want to exist.

i want to be nothing,

like i know i always have been.

...

how can i be in two states at the same time?

i tend to think of my state as a linear scale, from "good" to "bad". but i'm neither. i'm both.

i'm suffering, but i'm not suffering.

i want to live. i also want to not be alive.

sometimes they say there's different "voices" when it comes to internal thoughts - like, one who talks trash and one who comforts you. that isn't how it feels right now. it all just feels like me.

i can't decide where i want to go. all i can do right now is keep throwing myself back into the water.

...

i'm going to bed
